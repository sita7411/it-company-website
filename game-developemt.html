FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Game development is the process of designing, creating, and producing interactive games for various platforms like mobile, PC, consoles, or the web. It involves combining art, programming, storytelling, sound design, and user experience to create engaging and entertaining digital experiences.

Game development is a complex process that often encounters challenges such as performance optimization, platform compatibility, and balancing engaging gameplay. Developers must manage technical limitations, tight deadlines, and team collaboration while ensuring the final product meets user expectations.

We follow a well-defined game development process to ensure quality, creativity, and timely delivery. It starts with planning and concept development, where we define the game idea, platform, features, and audience. Next, we create a prototype to test gameplay mechanics and user experience. Our artists then design the required 2D/3D assets, animations, and sounds. In the development stage, we build the full game using engines like Unity or Unreal Engine. We thoroughly test for bugs, performance, and device compatibility. After launch, we provide post-release support including updates and optimizations.

The cost of game development depends on the type, complexity, and platform. A basic 2D mobile game may cost between ₹50,000 to ₹2 lakhs, while more complex 3D or multiplayer games can range from ₹5 lakhs to ₹50 lakhs or more. Pricing is influenced by factors like game design, number of levels, platform support, animations, backend features, and post-launch services. We provide customized quotes based on your specific needs and budget.

We develop games for a wide range of platforms based on your target audience and game type. These include mobile platforms like Android and iOS, PC (Windows/macOS), web browsers using HTML5/WebGL, and consoles such as PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. We also build cross-platform games using engines like Unity and Unreal to ensure smooth performance across multiple devices. Whether it’s a single platform or multi-platform launch, we tailor the development process to meet your goals.

The development timeline depends on the game’s complexity and platform. Simple 2D games may take 1–3 months, while mid-level games need 4–6 months. Advanced 3D or multiplayer games can take 6–12+ months. We provide a custom timeline after reviewing your project requirements.
