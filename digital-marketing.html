FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Digital Marketing refers to the promotion of products or services using digital platforms like websites, search engines, social media, email, and online advertising. It helps businesses reach targeted audiences, improve brand visibility, and drive measurable results.

Common challenges in digital marketing include staying updated with algorithm changes, targeting the right audience, creating engaging content, managing multiple channels, and tracking performance across platforms. Budget management and competition are also key concerns.

Our digital marketing process starts with understanding your goals and audience. We then create a customized strategy that includes SEO, social media, paid ads, content marketing, and email automation. Regular performance tracking and optimization ensure maximum ROI.

Digital marketing costs vary based on project scope, platforms, and services involved. Small campaigns may start from ₹15,000/month, while comprehensive strategies with SEO, PPC, content, and email marketing can range from ₹50,000 to ₹2 lakhs/month or more.

We use a variety of platforms including Google (Search, Display, YouTube), Meta (Facebook, Instagram), LinkedIn, and email platforms like Mailchimp and HubSpot. We also work with SEO tools like Ahrefs and SEMrush to optimize your digital presence.

Results depend on the strategy and platform. SEO may take 3–6 months for significant impact, while paid ads and social media campaigns can start generating leads or traffic within a few days to weeks. Consistency and optimization are key to long-term success.
